About Me

Hi! My name is Steph. Im the face behind Mariposa Design. From a young age I have always loved drawing and creating, from oil pantings to my childrens halloween costumes, I am always making something. Recently I have been making custom AGA covers for my friends for christmas presents and they have convinced me to create a space where everyone is able to enjoy them. So please, keep scrolling to see my work and I hope you love them as much as I do. Each one is unique, hand drawn, painted, created and composed by myself and send to you! If you have any questions please do not hesitate to send me a message and I will reply to you as quick as I can.
